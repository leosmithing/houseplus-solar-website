# HousePlus Solar Series - GEO（生成式引擎优化）完整指南

## 🤖 什么是 GEO？

**GEO（Generative Engine Optimization）** 是针对 ChatGPT、Claude、Perplexity、Google Gemini 等生成式 AI 搜索引擎的优化策略。与传统 SEO 不同，GEO 专注于让 AI 引擎更容易理解、引用和推荐您的内容。

---

## 🎯 GEO 核心原则

### 1. 可发现性（Discoverability）
AI 引擎需要能够找到并索引您的内容。

**实施方法：**
- ✅ 提交 sitemap.xml
- ✅ 确保 robots.txt 允许 AI 爬虫
- ✅ 使用清晰的 URL 结构
- ✅ 包含内部链接

### 2. 可理解性（Comprehensibility）
AI 需要理解您的内容的含义和上下文。

**实施方法：**
- ✅ 使用清晰的标题层级（H1 → H2 → H3）
- ✅ 包含 Schema 结构化数据
- ✅ 使用描述性的 Alt 文本
- ✅ 避免模糊或歧义的表述

### 3. 权威性（Authority）
AI 倾向于引用来自权威来源的内容。

**实施方法：**
- ✅ 展示专业知识和经验
- ✅ 包含数据和统计
- ✅ 获得外部链接和引用
- ✅ 定期更新内容

### 4. 可信度（Trustworthiness）
AI 需要相信您的信息是准确和可靠的。

**实施方法：**
- ✅ 标注来源和参考文献
- ✅ 提供准确的联系信息
- ✅ 显示公司背景和认证
- ✅ 包含客户评价和案例研究

## 📝 GEO 最佳实践

### 1. 内容结构优化

#### 使用清晰的标题层级
```html
<h1>HousePlus Solar Series | Professional Solar Energy Solutions</h1>
<h2>Our Products</h2>
<h3>Solar Power Inverters</h3>
<h4>Features and Benefits</h4>
```

**为什么重要**：AI 使用标题来理解内容的层级结构和主题关系。

#### 编写清晰的摘要
每个部分应以清晰的摘要开始：
```markdown
## Solar Power Inverters

HousePlus Solar Power Inverters are high-efficiency hybrid inverters designed for residential and commercial applications. They support three operating modes: grid-tie, off-grid, and hybrid, making them suitable for various energy scenarios.

### Key Features
- ...
```

#### 使用列表和表格
AI 更容易理解结构化的信息：
```markdown
### Product Comparison

| Feature | MPPT Controller | PWM Controller |
|---------|-----------------|----------------|
| Efficiency | 90-98% | 70-80% |
| Cost | Higher | Lower |
| Best For | Large systems | Small systems |
```

### 2. 常见问题（FAQ）优化

FAQ 是 GEO 的"金矿"。AI 搜索引擎特别喜欢 FAQ 格式的内容。

#### FAQ 最佳实践
```markdown
## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: What is the difference between MPPT and PWM solar charge controllers?

**A:** MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) controllers are more efficient than PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) controllers. Here's a detailed comparison:

- **MPPT Efficiency**: 90-98% - MPPT controllers continuously track the optimal power point of the solar panel, resulting in higher energy harvest.
- **PWM Efficiency**: 70-80% - PWM controllers use a simpler algorithm, suitable for smaller systems.
- **Cost**: MPPT controllers are more expensive but offer better long-term value.
- **System Size**: MPPT is recommended for systems above 400W, while PWM is suitable for smaller systems.

HousePlus offers both MPPT and PWM controllers to meet different customer needs.

### Q2: How long do lithium batteries last?

**A:** HousePlus lithium batteries (LiFePO4) are designed for longevity:
- **Lifespan**: 10-15 years with proper maintenance
- **Charge Cycles**: 3,000-5,000 cycles (compared to 500-1,000 for lead-acid)
- **Warranty**: 5-year manufacturer warranty
- **Degradation**: Less than 10% capacity loss after 10 years

This makes lithium batteries a cost-effective choice despite higher initial investment.
```

## 🔍 AI 搜索引擎友好的内容示例

### ❌ 不适合 GEO 的内容
```html
<div class="product">
  <img src="img1.jpg">
  <span>Great solar inverter!</span>
  <p>Best price ever</p>
</div>
```

**问题**：
- 缺少描述性的标题
- 没有 Alt 文本
- 信息不清晰
- 没有结构化数据

### ✅ 适合 GEO 的内容
```html
<article>
  <h1>HousePlus Solar Power Inverter 5KW - Hybrid Inverter with MPPT Charger</h1>
  
  <img src="solar-inverter-5kw.jpg" 
       alt="HousePlus 5KW hybrid solar inverter with built-in MPPT charger and UPS function">
  
  <p>The HousePlus 5KW Solar Power Inverter is a high-efficiency hybrid inverter designed 
     for residential and commercial solar systems. It supports three operating modes: 
     grid-tie, off-grid, and hybrid.</p>
  
  <h2>Key Features</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Efficiency: 93.5%</li>
    <li>Operating Modes: Grid-tie, Off-grid, Hybrid</li>
    <li>Built-in MPPT Charger: Yes</li>
    <li>Battery Type: Lead-acid or Lithium</li>
    <li>Warranty: 5 years</li>
  </ul>
  
  <h2>Technical Specifications</h2>
  <table>
    <tr><td>Power Range</td><td>1kW - 10kW</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Output Voltage</td><td>230VAC / 50-60Hz</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Efficiency</td><td>> 93%</td></tr>
  </table>
  
  <script type="application/ld+json">
  {
    "@type": "Product",
    "name": "HousePlus Solar Power Inverter 5KW",
    "description": "High-efficiency hybrid solar inverter...",
    "offers": {
      "@type": "AggregateOffer",
      "priceCurrency": "USD",
      "lowPrice": "2500",
      "highPrice": "3500"
    }
  }
  </script>
</article>
```

**优点**：
- 清晰的标题包含关键词
- 描述性的 Alt 文本
- 结构化的内容
- Schema 标记
- 技术规格表
