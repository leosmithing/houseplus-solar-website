About HousePlus Solar Series

Pioneering sustainable energy solutions for a better tomorrow

Our Story

Founded in 2003, HousePlus Solar Series has grown from a small regional manufacturer to a global leader in professional solar energy solutions. With over two decades of experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for residential, commercial, and industrial solar applications across more than 50 countries.

Our mission is to make renewable energy accessible, affordable, and reliable for everyone. We believe that solar power is not just an alternative energy source—it's the future of sustainable development. Every product we manufacture is designed with this vision in mind.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower the world with innovative, reliable, and cost-effective solar energy solutions that drive sustainable development and energy independence.

💡 Our Vision

To become the most trusted and respected solar energy solutions provider globally, recognized for quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
