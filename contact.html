Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

We accommodate orders of all sizes, from single units to bulk shipments. For large orders, we offer special pricing and customization options.

Do you offer OEM/ODM services?

Yes! We provide comprehensive OEM/ODM services including custom design, private labeling, and bulk manufacturing. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

What are your payment terms?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfer, PayPal, and other international payment solutions. Payment terms can be negotiated based on order volume and customer creditworthiness.

How long is the warranty on your products?

Most of our products come with 2-10 year warranties depending on the product type. Lithium batteries typically have 10-year warranties, while inverters have 5-year warranties.

Do you provide technical support?

Absolutely! Our technical team is available 24/7 to assist with installation, troubleshooting, and optimization of your solar systems.

What is your delivery timeframe?

Standard delivery is 7-14 days for stock items. Custom orders may take 2-4 weeks depending on specifications. We work with reliable logistics partners to ensure timely delivery worldwide.
